Welcome to Kanban Board

Below you will find instructions on how to request the removal of your data from our system.

  1. Open Telegram app.
  2. Go to our channel Kanban Board.
  3. Send a message to the channel requesting the removal of your data. Please include your User ID if possible.

Data Collection and Storage

We collect and store the following types of data:

  • Financial Info: Adapty collects purchase history from users.
  • Personal Info and Identifiers: Adapty collects User ID and some other identifiable contact information including name, email address, phone number, etc., if you explicitly pass them to Adapty SDK.
  • Device and other identifiers: Adapty collects data on device id.

We aim to delete all your data within 30 days of receiving your removal request.
